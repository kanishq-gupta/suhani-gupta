Overview

Portulaca grandiflora is a stunning annual flowering plant native to Argentina, Uruguay, and southern Brazil. Known for its vibrant, colorful blooms and succulent-like leaves, this hardy plant has become a favorite in gardens worldwide.

Characteristics

  • Height: 10-30 cm (4-12 inches)
  • Flower colors: Pink, red, yellow, white, orange
  • Blooming period: Summer to early autumn
  • Succulent, cylindrical leaves
  • Flowers open in sunlight, close at night

Habitat & Growing

Thrives in full sunlight and well-drained, sandy soil. Extremely drought-tolerant and heat-resistant. Perfect for rock gardens, containers, and as ground cover. Requires minimal water once established.

Care Tips
