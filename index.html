Portulaca grandiflora comes from South America, especially from Brazil, Argentina, and Uruguay. It grows best in warm, sunny places and is now loved all around the world for its bright flowers and easy care.

Where It Grows Best

Portulaca grandiflora grows best in sunny places. It likes dry soil that lets water pass easily (well-drained soil). This plant does not need much water and is perfect for hot weather. You can grow it in pots, gardens, or near windows where there is sunlight.

Plant Care Tips

Portulaca grandiflora is very easy to take care of. It should be kept in a sunny place where it gets at least 4 to 6 hours of sunlight every day. Water the plant only when the soil feels dry. Make sure the soil drains well so that water does not collect. You should remove dry or dead flowers so that new flowers can grow easily.
